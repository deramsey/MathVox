Keyboard help for the equation field

You don't need to reach the small keyboard/menu icons in the corner of the equation field to use them — the same actions are available as keyboard shortcuts from anywhere inside the field:

  • Shift+Alt+Space (Shift+Option+Space on Mac) — show or hide the on-screen math keyboard
  • Alt+Space (Option+Space on Mac) — open the field's options menu
  • Tab / Shift+Tab — jump to the next/previous placeholder (e.g. into or out of a fraction)
  • Arrow keys — move through the expression; Space moves out of a group (like out of an exponent)
  • / for a fraction, ^ for an exponent, _ for a subscript, or type a backslash (\) to enter raw LaTeX at the cursor
  • Alt+Ctrl+↓ — have the whole expression read aloud from inside the field (same as the Read Equation Aloud button below)

Prefer not to use the visual editor at all? Type or paste LaTeX directly into the text box below and select Convert — that loads it into the equation field and updates every output format.

Example: e=mc^2. Useful for fixing things like braces that are hard to edit in the visual field above. Select Convert (or press Ctrl+Enter) to apply it.
